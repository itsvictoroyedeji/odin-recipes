Quinoa Chickpea Wrap

Description

Who said quinoa wasn't good? Let's make a chickpea wrap with quinoa today. Both the chickpeas and quinoa are a great source of protein, so you're in good hands with this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Royal Quinoa
  • Can of Chickpeas
  • Joe's Kansas City Seasoning Salt
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • 1 Lemon
  • Organic Tahini
  • Large Flour Tortillas (for each wrap)
  • Red Onions
  • Bell Peppers
  • Habanera Peppers
  • Ginger
  • 2 packs of 3.5oz Shiitake Mushrooms
  • Organic Tomato Paste
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Minced Garlic
  • Organic Agave Sweetener
  • Romaine Lettuce

Steps

  1. Add your Quinoa to rice cooker
  2. Add garbanzo beans (chickpeas)
  3. Prepare lemon sauce dressing
  4. Wait for Chickpeas and Quinoa to finish
  5. Assemble the wrap
  6. Fold the wrap
