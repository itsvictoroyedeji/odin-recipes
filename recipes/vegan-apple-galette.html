Vegan Apple Galette

Description

2 Granny Smith and 2 Honey Crisp apples. That's all you need. Both are rich in fiber! Vegan Apple Galette with Puff Pastry recipe is below.

Ingredients

  • Puff Pastry Sheets
  • 2 Granny Smith Apples
  • 2 Honey Crisp Apples
  • 1 Lemon
  • Vanilla extract
  • Agave sweetener
  • Ground Cinnamon
  • Kosher Salt
  • 100% pure Corn Starch
  • Unsweetened Silk plant-based milk

Steps

  1. Prepare and cut the apples
  2. Season the apples
  3. Prepare Pull pastry
  4. Add the apples to the pastry
  5. Fold over pastry edges
  6. Brush the pastry edges
  7. Add Galette to oven
  8. Cut the Galette
