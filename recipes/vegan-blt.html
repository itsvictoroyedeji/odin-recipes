Vegan BLT

Description

Yes, there are healthy BLTs out there! Learn how to make a Vegan BLT using homemade tomatoes that we grew from our garden.

Ingredients

  • 3 pcs LiteLife Smart Bacon
  • Tomatoes
  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Sourdough bread
  • Miyokos Creamery 'Smoked English' Farmhouse cheese
  • Guacamole
  • Red Pepper Hummus
  • Bagel Sesame Seasoning Blend
  • Kosher Dill Pickles

Steps

  1. Add bacons to air fryer
  2. Prepare tomatoes, lettuce, and bread
  3. Flip over bacon
  4. Prepare vegan cheese and other veggies
  5. Cut the sandwich
